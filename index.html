Mystic Mod

Welcome to Mystic Mod! Mystic mod is a recreation of Sam Hogan's Cell Machine. Made by Mystic#8374 and WorstPacifist#8024, Mystic Mod fixes many issues and introduces a number of new features into the simple game.
